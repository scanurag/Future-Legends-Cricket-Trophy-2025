WHO CAN REGISTER?

The registration process is quite simple. Are you above 09 years of age? If yes, this is the beginning of your career in cricket. You can get registration with only Rs 950/-. This will give you a chance to play for the trials. These trials are held all over India in 16 states.

SELECTION PROCESS

Three State League Matches will be played in T-20 Format at LEVEL 2(Selection Matches). All Matches will be Live(only scoring) on CricHero App. All Matches Will be Broadcasted live ( Level 3 Matches) on Youtube, Facebook, Instagram & News Channels. There are many Scholarships, Medals/ Certificates & Attractive Prizes to be won.

OUR AIM

To give you an opportunity to show your talent at National Level in India. >To prepare you for world class cricket. >To give you exposure with experienced Coaches. >To help you to get noticed by BCCI. >To support you in making career in cricket. >To give fair chance of selection based on performance.
